Executive Summary of Digital Agency Teams

This report provides a comprehensive overview of five digital agency teams participating in the Digital Business Simulation course for 2025. Each team represents a unique approach to digital marketing and business solutions, showcasing diverse specializations from social media advertising to comprehensive digital transformation services.
